Quim Motger

I am a PhD student from the Computing PhD program at Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya (BarcelonaTech). I am a member of the Software and Service Engineering Research Group (GESSI). My research is focused in the field of AI-based software systems, natural language processing and advanced, contextualized conversational agents.
